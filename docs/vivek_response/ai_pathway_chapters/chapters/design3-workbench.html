# AI Concepts
Three mental models for chain-of-thought prompting
The Prediction Problem
LLMs predict the next token based on statistical patterns — they don't "reason." Without structured steps, they jump to plausible-sounding conclusions that skip real analytical work. This is why direct questions about complex topics produce confident but shallow outputs.
ROOT CAUSE
The Chain Mechanism
CoT forces the model to generate intermediate reasoning steps before concluding. Critically: each generated reasoning step becomes part of the context for the next step. The model is literally using its own reasoning as input — so quality compounds.
HOW IT WORKS
Your Marketing Payoff
For B2B marketing work: CoT is the difference between a generic campaign outline and a brief that references your ICP, accounts for competitive positioning, and channels strategy based on your actual Q2 performance. One prompt. Board-ready output.
